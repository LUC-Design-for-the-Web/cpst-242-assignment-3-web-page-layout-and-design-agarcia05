Welcome to the Crested Gecko Website!

This website is all about the crested gecko, a popular pet reptile. Here you can learn about crested gecko care, diet, habitat, and more.

Gecko Facts

  • Crested geckos are native to New Caledonia.
  • They are arboreal, meaning they live in trees.
  • They have large eyes and a crest on their heads.
  • They can change color to camouflage themselves.
  • They are omnivores and eat insects, fruits, and vegetables.

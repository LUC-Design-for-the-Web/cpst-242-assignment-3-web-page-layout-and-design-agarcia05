Crested Gecko Care Guide

Housing

Proper housing is crucial for the well-being of your crested gecko. Here are some housing tips:

  • Use a vertically oriented terrarium to mimic their natural habitat.
  • Provide branches and foliage for climbing and hiding.
  • Maintain proper humidity and temperature levels.

Health

Ensuring the health of your crested gecko is vital. Here are some health guidelines:

  • Feed them a balanced diet of fruit-based crested gecko diet and insects.
  • Keep their enclosure clean to prevent infections and parasites.
  • Regularly check for signs of stress, illness, or injury.
